Higher Education Support
The Centre for Higher Education at Sri Eshwar College of Engineering supports students who want to
pursue postgraduate education such as MS, MBA, or PhD in foreign universities. The centre provides
structured guidance and preparation programs for global education opportunities.
Core Activities
Overseas Education Guidance
The centre helps students understand global education pathways, including admission processes,
university selection, and scholarship opportunities.
