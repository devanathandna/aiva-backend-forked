Hostel Facilities – Sri Eshwar College of Engineering
Sri Eshwar College of Engineering provides on-campus hostel facilities for both boys and girls to
ensure a safe and comfortable living environment for students.
Accommodation
• Separate hostels are available for male and female students.
• The campus contains multiple hostel blocks with hundreds of rooms.
• Rooms are available in 2-sharing, 3-sharing, and 4-sharing options.
• Students can choose between attached bathroom and non-attached bathroom rooms.
Each hostel room is furnished with:
• Bed
• Study table
• Chair
• Wardrobe
• Internet access (Wi-Fi)
The hostel environment is designed to provide a home-like atmosphere for students who stay away
from their families.
Safety and Security
Student safety is a major priority in the hostel.
Security features include:
• 24-hour security and CCTV surveillance
• Strict hostel rules and attendance checks
• Controlled entry and exit timings
• Ragging-free campus environment
These measures help maintain a safe and disciplined living environment for students.
Hostel Amenities
The hostel provides several facilities to support daily student life.
Major amenities include:
• High-speed Wi-Fi internet
• Gym and fitness facilities
• Laundry services
• Recreation areas and common rooms
• Medical assistance and first aid facilities
• Water purifiers and drinking water systems
These facilities ensure that students have both academic and personal comfort while staying in the
hostel.
Food and Mess Facilities
The hostel provides mess facilities that serve regular meals for students.
Mess Facilities
• The hostel campus has multiple mess halls with large seating capacity.
• Students are served nutritious and hygienic meals daily.
• Meals are available at fixed timings such as breakfast, lunch, and dinner.
Types of Food
The mess provides a variety of cuisines including:
• South Indian food
• North Indian food
• Chinese dishes
• Snacks and beverages
Food is prepared to provide balanced nutrition for students living on campus.
Cafeteria and Additional Food Options
Apart from the hostel mess, the campus also includes canteens and cafeterias where students can
buy snacks, beverages, and quick meals.
The cafeteria has a large seating capacity and offers a variety of food options for students throughout
the day.
Overall Hostel Experience
The hostel life at Sri Eshwar College provides students with:
• Comfortable accommodation
• Safe and secure environment
• Nutritious food and dining facilities
• Opportunities to interact with fellow students
• Access to recreation and study spaces
Hostel life helps students develop independence, teamwork, and lifelong friendships while focusing
on their academic journey
