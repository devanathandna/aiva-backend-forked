1. Overview
Sri Eshwar College of Engineering (SECE) is an engineering institution located in Kondampatti,
Vadasithur via Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, India. The college provides engineering education with a focus
on industry-ready skills, research, innovation, and career development. It aims to produce globally
competent engineers through value-based education and modern learning facilities.
The institution emphasizes strong industry connections, innovation, and practical learning to prepare
students for global technology careers.
2. Vision and Mission
Vision
To be recognized as a premier institution that develops students into globally acknowledged
engineering professionals.
Mission
• Provide outcome-based and value-based engineering education.
• Encourage research and entrepreneurial culture.
• Prepare students to become industry-ready professionals.
• Develop leadership and behavioral skills.
• Promote social responsibility among students.
