Institutional Achievements
Sri Eshwar College of Engineering has received several awards and recognitions for excellence in
engineering education, industry collaboration, and student development.
• The institution received the Best Industry Linked Emerging Engineering Institute Award from
AICTE-CII in 2014 among 952 engineering colleges in India.
• The college was honored with the Best Placement and Career Building Award by the All India
Technical and Management Council during the 3rd Annual Corporate Summit.
• Sri Eshwar College received the Sustainable Institute Industry Partnership Award from the
Society for Educational and Entrepreneurship Development (SEED) under the category of
upcoming engineering colleges with strong potential.
• The institution was recognized with the Best Industry Interface Institute Award by the 24MRC
Network in 2015.
• The college received the Best Organization Encouraging Entrepreneurs in MSME Award from
ASSOCHAM in 2015.
• Sri Eshwar College secured the 9th rank in the DATAQUEST Top T-Schools Survey in 2016.
• The college was also awarded the National Level Award for Best Professional Institute for
Skill Development by the Honorable Union Minister Rajeev Pratap Rudi.
• The principal of the institution, Dr. Sudha Mohanram, received the Icon of Coimbatore Award
from Rathinam Group of Institutions.
• The institution has also received recognition for sustainability initiatives such as the Igen
Green Day Award and SDG Action Award 2024.
