Sri Eshwar College of Engineering (SECE) - Admissions Information
=================================================================

Admission to Sri Eshwar College of Engineering (SECE) in Coimbatore is primarily conducted through the TNEA (Tamil Nadu Engineering Admissions) process for undergraduate courses and GATE/CEETA for postgraduate programs.

---

Undergraduate Admissions (B.E. / B.Tech)
-----------------------------------------

Admission for first-year engineering is based on performance in the Higher Secondary (+2) examinations and follows the Single Window Counselling by the Directorate of Technical Education (DoTE), Chennai.

Selection Process:
- Selection is based on the TNEA Rank List and subsequent counselling.
- Admissions are conducted through TNEA counselling (Tamil Nadu Engineering Admissions).
- Admissions are NOT based on merit alone; they follow the TNEA counselling process.

Important Dates (2026):
- Registration Window: Expected to open in the first week of May 2026.
- Counselling Dates: General counselling typically runs from mid-July to mid-August.

Lateral Entry:
- Candidates with a 3-year diploma or B.Sc. can join the 2nd year directly.

---

Postgraduate Admissions (M.E. / M.Tech)
-----------------------------------------

Admission to postgraduate courses is entrance-based.

Entrance Exams:
- Valid scores in GATE or CEETA (formerly TANCET) are required.

Eligibility:
- Graduation with a minimum of 50% aggregate.

---

Application Process
--------------------

1. Obtain Form: Download the application from the Official SECE Website or apply at the college admission office.
2. Register for Counselling: Simultaneously register for TNEA (for UG) or TANCA (for PG).
3. Submission: Submit the filled application along with required documents:
   - Aadhaar Card
   - Marksheets
   - Community Certificate
   - Transfer Certificate
4. Confirmation: If selected through counselling, visit the campus for document verification and fee payment to secure the seat.

---

Scholarships
-------------

SECE offers merit-based fee concessions:

- 100% Tuition Waiver: For cut-offs of 190 and above.
- 75% Tuition Waiver: For cut-offs between 188–189.75.
- 50% Tuition Waiver: For cut-offs between 180–187.75.
- Government Scholarships: Available for SC/ST and first-generation graduate students.
